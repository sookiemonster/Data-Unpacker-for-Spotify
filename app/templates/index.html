ABOUT

Data Unpacker for Spotify is a web application that visualizes the user data package provided to Spotify listeners. It was created on July 12th, 2021 with the intent of developing an easy and intuitive way to view trends in our listening tendencies and specific information about those trends.

This application was built with built with HTML, CSS, JQuery, Jinja & Flask. It also implements Chart.js for graphs.

Within this application you can upload your data package to view your top 10 most listened to songs & artists, how many times you've listened to them & when you tend to listen to them over a given interval. You can also observe those statistics for music in general.


NOTE: While this is more in-depth than many other applications that utilize Spotify's recently played music, the data package still only dates back a year, so it may not be fully representative of your entire Spotify listening history.
